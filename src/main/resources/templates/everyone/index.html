How to play Trivia King?
Welcome to the Trivia Game! Test your knowledge and see how many questions you can answer correctly.

Here's how to play:
* Read each question carefully and select the answer you think is correct.
* Click the 'Submit' button to submit your answer.
* Try to answer as many questions as you can to reach the end.
* Your goal is to earn a high score and make it to the top 10 in the score table.

Scoring System:
* For each correct answer, you will earn 7.5 points.
* If your answer is incorrect, you will lose.
* At the end of the game, your total score will be calculated and recorded in the score table.
* Try to answer correctly to maximize your score!

Score Table:
* You can view the top scores achieved by other players in the score table.
* The score table displays the usernames of the top 10 players and their respective scores.
* To view the score table, click on the 'View Scores' button in the game page.
* Your goal is to achieve a high score and secure a place among the top 10 players!

Good luck and enjoy the game!
